About Us

Du-All Safety, LLC established in 1989 is a full-service environmental health and safety firm located in Fremont, California. Du-All Safety provides support services to businesses and municipalities to comply with Federal, State, and local EH&S requirements. Du-All Safety employs subject-matter experts on all EH&S topics who provide quality employee training and consulting. Services include: site safety inspections, written safety program assessments/development, hands-on OSHA training, online safety training, environmental health programs, permits, hazardous materials and waste management, and much more.
